About

This web app uses a deep convolutional neural network to classify shoes from 14 popular running shoe brands. It predicts the top three likely brands represented in the image. The model is trained on ~10k total images gathered online from publicly available data and achieves 91% accuracy on the validation set. Future versions of the model aim to gather more images taken from phones and at obscure angles to improve the robustness of the model.
